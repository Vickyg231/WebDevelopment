IntroPage

Welcome to the game of Extreme Rock Paper Scissors! The goal of the game is to get all the weapons from the computer! You will start with 5 weapons of each type. Some important things to keep in mind is if you use a weapon consecutively for three times, the weapon will be "broken" or removed from your total. If you have zero amount of a weapon, you will not be able to play the weapon.If the computer and you tie 3 consecutive times, you and the computer will lose a random weapon. Have fun!
